Abstract: One or two interviews will be hold with people initiating a startup based on Free Software. Some issues will be foregrounded, as, in example:

- Advantages and disadvantages on a Startup due to FLOSS use.

- Advantages and disadvantages on a Startup due to FLOSS production.

- Considerations to be taken into account when initiating a startup regarding Free Software:

  * Potential customers

  * Comunity involvment

  * Positioning: Decreasing costs or improving services

  * etc.

- Plans to fork to propietary software.

- Economic impact expected due to community involvement

- etc.

Some derived questions from issues above would be proposed to the interviewed people, to try to clarify economic aspects on startups with a Free Software based mod
